Help fix the world of Super Mario through a series of coding challenges!

Where: ESI,Oued smar

When: 28 Nov ~ 31 Nov


Super Coder World is a fun game themed event in the world of Super Mario! Participants become coders on a quest to save the digital world from a system crash.

To progress through levels, they must solve coding challenges, logic puzzles and debugging missions, each representing a zone of the broken system.




Who are we?

About CSE...

We are the Scientific Club of ESI (CSE) in Algiers. Since 2008, we’ve been organizing hackathons, workshops, conferences, and crazy coding events to level up the Algerian tech community

World 1 – The Kingdom of Algorithms

Welcome to a lush green kingdom where floating platforms are data structures and green pipes hide algorithms to optimize! Logical Goombas and recursive Koopas stand in your way. The 5 challenges test the core of competitive programming: - Classic & advanced data structures - Standard algorithms - Time/space optimization - Intense debugging and bug hunting

×

World 2 – The AI & Data Science Labyrinth

The scenery turns dark and mysterious: data nebulae, predictive ghosts, and probabilistic Boos lurk everywhere. This world dives deep into modern AI: - Dataset cleaning & creative feature engineering - Building models (regression, classification, clustering, light NLP & computer vision) - Live predictions and “magical” challenges (image generation, basic RL)

×

World 3 – Bowser’s Castle: Capture The Flag – Cybersecurity

The grand finale! Teams storm Bowser’s dark castle guarded by malicious Lakitus and cryptographic Thwomps. A full-intensity CTF with 8 major challenges: - Web (SQLi, XSS, SSRF, IDOR...) - Crypto (RSA, AES, hash cracking...) - Reverse engineering & crackmes - Forensics & steganography - Pwn (buffer overflows, ROP...) - OSINT & misc
